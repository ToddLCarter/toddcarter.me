About Me

My interest for software engineering developed at a young age through online games where I was exposed to many technologies that I had not previously encountered. The freedom presented to developers and what programming enabled them to accomplish established my fascination for software engineering resulting in my comprehensive understanding of numerous backend and frontend programming languages: Java, Go (Golang), Node.js, HTML, CSS, and SQL.

For the last 5 years I have gained an extensive amount of knowledge on system administration, security, programming and customer service working on personal projects. The projects developed my passion for computing inspiring me to become a self-taught Java, Go (Golang) & Node.js developer. My connection with these projects have led me to meet several skilled individuals who I have collaborated with to develop applications. These partnerships refined my knowledge on the development life cycle and how to successfully deploy, debug and monitor products in a production environment.

A lot of these applications need to be have high availability. Therefore high performance and scalability were key factors during the designing process. To meet this, I used Kubernetes to deploy Docker containers across various cloud instances as well as the use of Redis and RabbitMQ to allow cross-communication between the distributed instances. As well as SQL (MySQL, SQLite) and No-SQL (MongoDB) databases for permanent storage of data within the applications.
